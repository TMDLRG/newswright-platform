## 1. Executive Summary

This pilot plan executes a scaled 50-site validation strategy using staggered cohorts to maximize learning velocity while managing operational complexity. The contracted PM model ($5,500/mo) enables professional onboarding and testimonial collection without pulling founders into logistics.

---

## 2. PROVE Framework — Pilot Hypothesis

### 2.1 Prediction

Within 90 days, target newsrooms will demonstrate:

- Time savings: ≥40% reduction in editorial workflow time for AI-augmented content types
- Quality validation: ≥80% AI draft approval rate with ≤2 revisions
- Adoption willingness: ≥40 of 50 sites (80%) willing to continue post-pilot
- Testimonial success: ≥10 testimonials (written or video) secured
- Platform stability: Zero ethics violations; 99.5%+ uptime under 50-site load

### 2.2 Validation — Gate 1 Criteria

**PASS if ALL met at Day 90:**

| Criterion | Target | Confidence |
|-----------|--------|-----------|
| AI draft approval rate | ≥80% with ≤2 revisions | High |
| Time savings documented | ≥40% per workflow | High |
| Testimonials secured | ≥10 (written or video) | Medium |
| Sites willing to continue | ≥40 of 50 (80%) | Medium |
| Ethics violations | Zero | High |
| Platform stability (uptime) | 99.5%+ under 50-site load | High |

**FAIL triggers (any one):** Approval rate <60%; Time savings <25%; Any ethics violation published; Platform instability; <30 sites willing to continue (<60%)
