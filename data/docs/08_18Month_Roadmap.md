## 4. Phase Details & OKRs

### Phase 0: Scaled Pilot (M1–3)

**Focus:** Validate product-market fit with 50 newsrooms across 3 staggered cohorts
**Budget:** $154,318 (incl. 10% risk reserve)
**Gate:** Gate 1 at Day 90 — ≥80% approval, ≥40% time savings, ≥10 testimonials

**Objective 1: Validate Core Product**
- KR1: ≥80% AI draft approval rate with ≤2 revisions
- KR2: ≥40% time savings documented across pilot sites
- KR3: Zero journalism ethics violations

**Objective 2: Build Conversion Pipeline**
- KR1: 50 sites onboarded across 3 cohorts
- KR2: ≥40 sites willing to continue (80%)
- KR3: ≥10 testimonials secured

### Phase 1: Pilot Conversions (M4–6)

**Focus:** Convert pilot sites to paid; activate founder compensation
**Target:** 35 clients, $34K MRR by M6

**Pricing:** Core $249/mo | Pro $499/mo | Premium $749/mo | Enterprise $1,249/mo

**Objective 1: Convert Pilot to Revenue**
- KR1: 29 pilot sites convert (58% avg)
- KR2: 25 paying clients by M5 (trigger comp)
- KR3: $34K MRR by M6

**Objective 2: Establish Operations**
- KR1: GM hired and onboarded (M4)
- KR2: Production brand delivered ($20K)
- KR3: CS Manager hired (M5)

### Phase 2: Partner Scale + Series Seed (M7–12)

**Focus:** Scale via partner channels; close Series Seed
**Target:** 92 clients, $60K MRR, $720K ARR by M12

**Objective 1: Scale Client Base**
- KR1: 50+ paying clients by M8
- KR2: 8–14 new partner clients/month
- KR3: 92 clients, $60K MRR by M12

**Objective 2: Secure Series Seed**
- KR1: Series Seed closed by M10
- KR2: $1.0–$1.5M at $8–$10M pre-money
- KR3: 12+ months runway post-close

### Phase 3: Cash Flow Positive (M13–18)

**Focus:** Achieve break-even; prepare for scale
**Target:** 176 clients, $102K MRR by M18

**Objective 1: Achieve Profitability**
